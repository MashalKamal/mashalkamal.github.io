Welcome to Gharzai Brand

Gharzai presents a new collection of handmade clothing, created with care and tradition by Afghan women working from their homes. Each piece reflects generations of craftsmanship, cultural heritage, and the unique artistry of Afghan handwork.

Gharzai Brand is more than fashion â€” it is a source of income and independence for widowed women and women who are the heads of their households. Through home-based businesses, these women are able to support their families with dignity, using their skills in embroidery, stitching, and traditional textile design.

Gharzai provides a safe and supportive platform where women do not have to search for customers on their own. Instead, the brand connects their handmade clothing to a wider market, allowing their work to be seen, valued, and appreciated. By choosing Gharzai, you support Afghan women artisans, preserve traditional craftsmanship, and help create sustainable livelihoods.

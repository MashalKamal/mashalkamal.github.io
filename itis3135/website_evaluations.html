Well-Designed Website

Amazon

  • Contrast: The text is easy to read because it stands out against the background.
  • Repetition: The same fonts and colors are used throughout the site, making it easy to recognize.
  • Alignment: Everything is lined up well, giving the site a tidy look.
  • Proximity: Related items are close together, which helps with navigation.

Poorly Designed Website

Terrible Website

  • Contrast: The text is hard to read because it blends into the background.
  • Repetition: Different designs and styles make the site confusing.
  • Alignment: Things are not lined up properly, making it look messy.
  • Proximity: Unrelated items are mixed together, making it hard to find what you need.

Afghanistan has a rich cultural heritage shaped by Central Asian, Persian, South Asian, and Middle Eastern influences. Clothing in Afghanistan is not just about fashion—it reflects identity, ethnicity, region, religion, and tradition.

Discover handmade clothing created by Afghan women. Afghani cloth culture is deeply tied to tradition and identity. More than just what people wear, these clothes show history, pride, and cultural values passed down through generations.

About Mehr Shop

Celebrating Culture, Craftsmanship & Modern Fashion

Our Story

Mehr Shop was created to celebrate traditional Middle Eastern and South Asian fashion, bringing cultural elegance into modern clothing styles.

Women in Fashion

Many of our designs are created by skilled women artisans who bring creativity, craftsmanship, and cultural heritage into every piece.

Handmade Clothing

A large part of our collection is handmade using detailed embroidery and traditional techniques, making each outfit unique and high quality.

Custom Orders

We offer custom-made outfits based on your design, size, and color preferences. Our team works closely with you to create a unique piece tailored just for you.

Special Order Process

Customers can describe their desired style, size, and color in detail. After submitting your request, our customer service team will contact you by phone to confirm all order details and ensure accuracy.

We also welcome in-person visits where our team can take your measurements and help you choose the perfect fit.
